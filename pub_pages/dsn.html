Schrödinger's CRCs (Fast Abstract)

  • ABSTRACT:

    I revisit the fault-tolerance of cyclic redundancy checks (CRCs), expanding on the work of Driscoll et al. I introduce the concepts of Schrödinger-Hamming weight and Schr\"{o}dinger-Hamming distance, and I argue that under a fault model in which stuck-at-one-half or slightly-out-of-spec faults dominate, current methods for computing the fault detection of CRCs may be over-optimistic.
